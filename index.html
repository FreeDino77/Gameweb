Chemflora & Fauna

Nature engineered. Beauty corrupted.

About the Mod

Chemflora & Fauna introduces an ecosystem where beauty and biohazard coexist. Gentle creatures and glowing plants thrive by day — until nightfall reveals their toxic side. Watch the line blur between life and contamination.

Features

  • ☀️ Passive, bioluminescent mobs during daylight
  • 🌙 Nighttime mutations and corrupted transformations
  • 💨 Custom chemical particle emissions and status effects
  • 🧪 Interactive flora that react to environment changes
  • 🔬 Expandable system for future mutated species
